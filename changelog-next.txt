- Added Cardboard Box:
  - Similar block to existing Basket (Shulker Box like with only 5 slots).
  - Crafted with 4 Planks, 4 Papers and 1 Slime Ball.
- You can now tie container items (like Shulker Boxes, Cardboard Boxes, itp) to hide their contents.
  - This only hides to item list in tooltip.
  - You can do it by crafting the container item with a String.
  - The "Tied" effect is removed once container is opened.
- Added Burning Ghost Light and Copper Ghost Light:
  - Crafted with Coal / Copper Nugged and regular Ghost Light.
- Right-clicking Basket (and Cardboard Box) allows to force it to be open (similar to Trash Can).
- Using Brush on edge of the canvas will now draw on neighboring canvases.
